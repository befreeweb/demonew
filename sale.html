Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the BOC Chain token sale

What is BOC Chain and what problem does it solve?

BOC Chain is a specialized blockchain platform designed for the entertainment industry. It solves key problems like content monetization, creator-fan engagement, and digital asset ownership by providing tools for tokenizing content, building communities, and enabling direct monetization without intermediaries.

How can I participate in the BOC token sale?

To participate, connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.), choose your payment method (ETH, USDT, USDC, or BNB), enter your purchase amount (minimum $50), and confirm the transaction. Tokens are delivered instantly to your wallet.

What are the current token sale phases and pricing?

We're currently in Phase 3 (Public Sale) at $0.18 per BOC token with a 10% bonus. Phase 1 (Seed) was $0.12, Phase 2 (Private) was $0.15, and the final Phase 4 will be $0.22. The listing price will be $0.25.

What is the vesting schedule for purchased tokens?

Public sale tokens have 25% released at TGE (Token Generation Event), then 25% monthly for 3 months. Private sale tokens have 10% at TGE, then 15% monthly for 6 months. Team tokens have a 6-month cliff with quarterly releases over 2 years.

When will BOC tokens be listed on exchanges?

BOC tokens will be listed on major DEXs (Uniswap, PancakeSwap) immediately after TGE in Q3 2024. Major centralized exchange listings are planned within 30-60 days post-launch, with tier-1 exchanges targeted for Q4 2024.

What utility does the BOC token provide?

BOC tokens serve multiple purposes: paying platform fees, governance voting, staking for rewards, accessing premium features, content monetization, NFT transactions, and participating in the creator economy ecosystem.

Is there a minimum or maximum purchase limit?

The minimum purchase amount is $50 USD equivalent in any supported cryptocurrency. There is no maximum limit for individual purchases during the public sale phase.

How secure is the token sale smart contract?

Our smart contracts have been audited by leading security firms including CertiK and Hacken. All audit reports are publicly available. The contracts use industry-standard security practices and have been tested extensively.
